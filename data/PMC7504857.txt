Huber Additional article information Correction to: J Biomed Sci 27, 64 In the original publication of this article, the word ‘biologys’ in the sentence ‘Therefore, Dictyostelium presents an excellent system to dissect the roles of SLC25A1 and SLC25A13 in NCL biologys.’ on Page 8 is incorrect. The correct sentence should be ‘Therefore, Dictyostelium presents an excellent system to dissect the roles of SLC25A1 and SLC25A13 in NCL pathology.’ Article information J Biomed Sci. 2020; 27: 94.
